Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-17

This Privacy Policy describes how 164 Investments, LLC ("Operator," "we," "us") handles data collected and processed through its internal QuickBooks Online integration software ("Software").

1. Scope of This Policy

The Software is a private, internal-use tool operated solely by 164 Investments, LLC and its affiliated limited liability companies ("Affiliated Entities") to administer QuickBooks Online accounts that we own or control. The Software is not offered to the public, is not distributed to third parties, and does not collect data from any individual other than the Operator's authorized personnel.

2. Information We Access

Through the QuickBooks Online API, the Software may access the following categories of information from QuickBooks Online accounts owned or controlled by the Operator:

  • Company profile information (company name, address, fiscal year settings)
  • Financial data (chart of accounts, general ledger, journal entries, invoices, bills, payments, purchases, transfers)
  • Standard financial reports (Profit & Loss, Balance Sheet, Cash Flow, Trial Balance, General Ledger, Transaction List)
  • Customer, vendor, and employee records as maintained in the QuickBooks Online account
  • Class, department, and location data used for internal categorization

The Software does not access, store, or transmit:

  • Payroll data beyond what is present in the general ledger
  • Payment card numbers or bank account credentials
  • Personal identifying information about individuals other than customers, vendors, and employees already recorded by the Operator in its QuickBooks Online accounts

3. How Information Is Used

Information accessed through the Software is used solely for:

  • Internal accounting, bookkeeping, and financial reporting
  • Preparation of federal, state, and local tax filings
  • Internal reporting to members and investors of Affiliated Entities
  • Reconciliation, audit, and compliance activities

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share information accessed through the Software with third parties, except:

  • With the Operator's certified public accountants, tax preparers, and legal counsel, solely for the purposes described above
  • As required by law, subpoena, or other valid legal process
  • With the explicit authorization of the owner of the applicable QuickBooks Online account

5. Data Storage and Security

Data retrieved by the Software is stored on systems controlled by the Operator. OAuth access and refresh tokens are stored locally and are not transmitted to any third party other than Intuit's QuickBooks Online API in the course of normal operation. Tokens may be revoked by the Operator at any time through the QuickBooks Online administrative console.

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect stored data, including restricted file system access, credential management, and network security controls appropriate for the Operator's scale.

6. Data Retention

Data retrieved through the Software is retained only as long as necessary for the purposes described in Section 3, or as required by applicable tax and corporate recordkeeping laws (typically seven years for tax records under IRS and Oregon Department of Revenue guidelines).

7. Your Rights

Because the Software processes data only from QuickBooks Online accounts owned or controlled by the Operator, rights of access, correction, or deletion under applicable privacy laws are exercised directly within QuickBooks Online by the account owner.

8. Children's Privacy

The Software is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this document reflects the current version.
